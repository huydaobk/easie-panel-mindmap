# EASIE SANDWICH PANEL SYSTEM ## A. Fundamentals & System Overview ### A.1 What is a Sandwich Panel? - Composite: 2 thin faces + 1 thick core - I-beam analogy: faces resist tension/compression, core resists shear - Up to 90% weight savings vs conventional steel ### A.2 Panel Types by Application - Wall panels (vertical load-bearing) - Roof panels (spanning, weatherproof) - Cladding panels (non-structural envelope) - Partitions / ceilings (interior) - Structural / frameless panels (load-bearing shell) ### A.3 Functional Layers - Face sheets (steel, aluminum, FRP) - Core (PUR, PIR, mineral wool, EPS) - Adhesive/bond layer - Joints & interfaces ### A.4 Performance Categories - Mechanical (strength, stiffness) - Thermal (insulation, U-value) - Airtightness / Watertightness - Fire (reaction & resistance) - Acoustic - Durability ## B. Materials & Component Properties ### B.1 Face Materials - Steel facings (280-320 MPa yield) - Coatings: galvanized, Aluzinc, HPS200, color-coated - Surface geometry: flat, ribbed, profiled, micro-rib ### B.2 Core Materials - **PUR/PIR Foam**: density 34-42 kg/m3, shear 0.04-0.14 MPa - **Mineral Wool (MW)**: density 100-125 kg/m3, shear 0.08 MPa, fire-resistant - **EPS**: economical, moisture-sensitive - **PVC/IPN (Divinycell H)**: high performance/weight ratio - **PET (Divinycell P)**: excellent thermal stability - **PES (Divinycell F)**: highest service temperature, fire/smoke performance ### B.3 Material Properties - Shear modulus & shear strength - Tensile/compressive strength - Wrinkling-related parameters - Creep & long-term behaviour - Thermal conductivity (lambda) ### B.4 Bond & Interface Behaviour - Adhesive types and curing - Bond quality indicators - Debonding failure modes ## C. Standards, Design Basis & Validation ### C.X DBT vs DBC (Design by Testing vs Design by Calculation) - **DBC**: standards-based analytical route (EN 14509 + ECCS-CIB) - **DBT**: test-based route to derive design resistance; complements DBC - Needs a **test matrix** to represent a product family (thickness/profile/core/fasteners) - EASIE reports **good correlation** and comparable safety levels - Sources: *Design-by-Testing---Calculation-of-Sandwich-Panels.pdf*; *EASIE PUBLISHABLE SUMMARY REPORT.pdf*; *European Recommendations for Sandwich Panels Part 1 Design. ECCSCIB Report - CIB Publication.pdf* ### C.1 EN 14509:2013 Framework - Factory-made self-supporting panels - CE Marking requirements - Factory Production Control (FPC) ### C.2 European Recommendations - ECCS-CIB Joint Committee guidance - Design-by-Calculation (DBC) - Design-by-Testing (DBT) ### C.3 Safety Philosophy - Partial factors (gamma_M, gamma_G, gamma_Q) - Reliability levels - Reference period ### C.4 Validation Routes - **DBC**: analytical calculation with declared properties - **DBT**: testing with statistical evaluation (k=0.85 for 1 test, 0.90 for 2 tests) - Parametric study: DBT vs DBC comparison ### C.5 Supporting Standards - EN 10143 (tolerances) - EN 10346 (coated steel) - EN 13162 (mineral wool) ## D. Structural Behaviour & Failure Mechanisms ### D.X Local failure and stiffness mechanics - **Wrinkling**: face-sheet local instability under compression - **Crippling**: localized instability near load introduction - **Core shear strength**: core-driven shear failure threshold - **Core shear modulus**: governs shear deformation share - **Creep coefficient**: long-term deformation amplifier ### D.1 Load Introduction & Load Paths - Point loads vs distributed loads - Support conditions: simple, continuous, cantilever - Load transfer through joints ### D.2 Global Panel Behaviour - Bending: face stresses, core shear - Shear deformation contribution - Composite action effectiveness - Deflection: bending + shear components ### D.3 Stability & Second-Order Effects - Global buckling - Local instability - P-Delta effects in frameless systems ### D.4 Connection Stiffness Influence - Joint stiffness on global behaviour - Semi-rigid modelling - Force distribution ### D.5 Failure Modes - **Face yielding**: plastic collapse - **Wrinkling**: local buckling of compressed face - **Shear buckling**: instability of core/face under shear - **Global buckling**: overall panel instability under compression - **Local vs global buckling interaction**: stiffened panels may fail globally - **Core shear failure**: shear cracking/yielding - **Tensile failure in core**: rare but critical - **Debonding/delamination**: adhesive failure (face-core separation) - **Fastener/joint failures**: pull-through, pull-out, shear - **Local face yielding**: concentrated load / fastener pressure - **Local crushing**: support effects, point loads ### D.6 Limit States - **ULS**: Ultimate (strength, stability) - **SLS**: Serviceability (deflection L/200, vibration) - **FLS**: Fatigue (if applicable) ## E. # HỆ THỐNG PANEL SANDWICH EASIE ## A. Cơ Bản & Tổng Quan Hệ Thống ### A.1 Panel Sandwich là gì? - Vật liệu composite: 2 lớp mặt mỏng + 1 lõi dày - Tương tự dầm chữ I: mặt chịu kéo/nén, lõi chịu cắt - Tiết kiệm trọng lượng đến 90% so với thép thông thường ### A.2 Các Loại Panel Theo Ứng Dụng - Panel tường (chịu tải thẳng đứng) - Panel mái (vượt nhịp, chống thấm) - Panel ốp tường (vỏ bọc không chịu lực) - Panel ngăn / trần (nội thất) - Panel kết cấu / không khung (vỏ chịu lực) ### A.3 Các Lớp Chức Năng - Tấm mặt (thép, nhôm, FRP) - Lõi (PUR, PIR, bông khoáng, EPS) - Lớp keo/liên kết - Mối nối & các giao diện ### A.4 Các Hạng Mục Hiệu Năng - Cơ học (độ bền, độ cứng) - Nhiệt (cách nhiệt, hệ số U) - Kín khí / Chống thấm nước - Cháy (phản ứng & chống cháy) - Âm học - Độ bền ## B. Giai Đoạn 4-5: Kiến Thức Kỹ Thuật Sâu (Công Thức, Chi Phí, Quy Trình) ### N.1 Công Thức Nâng Cao - **Uốn kết hợp với creep**: `M^I = [M_W + M_ΔT + M_S(1+φ) + M_G(1+φ)] × α` - **Hệ số khuếch đại**: `α = 1 / (1 - N/N_cr)` - **Uốn ngang đàn hồi**: `N_cr = N_ki / (1 + N_ki/GA)` - **Độ cứng uốn panel**: `B_S = E_F · (A_F1·A_F2)/(A_F1+A_F2) · e²` - **Sức kháng liên kết trực tiếp**: `F_Rk = 4.2 · √(t_F² · d1) · f_u,F2` - **Ứng suất nhăn**: `σ_cr,w = 3/∛9 · ∛(2·E_I·G_C·E_C)` - **Hiệu chỉnh DBT**: `R_adj = R_obs · (f_y/f_y,obs)^α · (t/t_obs)^β` ### N.2 Ví Dụ Tính Toán D3.5 - Phân phối tải trục cho mặt - Ứng suất mặt (trục + uốn) -
